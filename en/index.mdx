Sawa is a social activity-planning platform. Users discover **places** and **activities**, connect through **companionships**, and plan collaborative **events** with voting, RSVP, and itinerary workflows.

This documentation is organized using the [Diataxis framework](https://diataxis.fr/): tutorials for learning, how-to guides for tasks, reference for facts, and explanation for architecture and concepts.
